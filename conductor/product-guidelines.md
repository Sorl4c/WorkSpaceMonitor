# Product Guidelines

## Design Philosophy
- **Minimalist & Distraction-Free:** Focus heavily on data density and structural layout without introducing visual clutter. The interface should disappear into the background to keep the user focused on their actual work.
- **Dark Mode First:** Optimized out of the box for low-light developer environments, ensuring that prolonged usage does not cause eye strain.

## Branding & Colors
- **Monochromatic & Grayscale Focus:** Strictly utilize neutral tones, grays, and muted shades to significantly reduce visual noise. Color should be used exceedingly sparingly, perhaps only to denote distinct states or critical errors.

## Tone of Voice & Copywriting
- **Restrained & Helpful:** The application should communicate in a clear, helpful, and restrained manner.
- **Concise Guidance:** Provide brief, practical messages that guide the user without being excessively cold or overly wordy.
- **Unobtrusive:** Avoid unnecessary text, interruptions, or pop-ups. Rely on intuitive design to communicate function wherever possible.

## User Interaction
- **Hover & Preview:** Prioritize rich tooltips and non-intrusive previews upon hovering. Allow users to quickly explore context (like peeking into virtual desktops or reading full window titles) without requiring clicks or active window switching.
